Performance Analysis

Based on the values of the two graphs, TCP is a more reliable source for sending data due to its ability to retransmit lost packets.

However, UDP is faster and more efficient for sending data. This can be shown on the speeds of the throughputs and RTTs of the 3 PCs to Network 1 and Network 2.

For low size files where the latency is also low, UDP is clearly superior and is able to send messages faster than TCP.
